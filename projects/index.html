[Project Love]

Every guy needs to do something for his lady-friend on Valentines' Day. The only question is what? Buy flowers? Naturally. Buy chocolates? Only the best imported directly from Belgium. But what else?

So of course at 1 am on Valentines' Day, I thought, why not make a website? Why not make a very simple website with 4 boxes? Each box would illustrate a word in "Will you be mine?" At 3 am, the juices begin flowing, and therefore, feature-creeping happens. I imagined a moving slideshow where it zooms in on each picture in an artsy way. I also imagined music playing.

[CampSummerset.com]

Camp Summerset is an incredible day-camp held in Chevy Chase, Maryland, that I had the pleasure of growing up with. Starting with 4 year old Mike attending MiniCamp, to 14 year old Mike becoming a "counselor-in-training," to 17 year old Mike becoming the "head counselor" (read: head honcho), to 18 year old Mike becoming a Teacher, to now 20 year old Mike being the sole Web Developer for the site, this camp has remained a part of my life.

After several years of a black and white pure HTML page (the 90s called, they want their design scheme back), the camp desperately needed a revision. I was approached by the wonderful directors to see if I was up to the task. I humbly accepted the torch and firmly believe I have lit the flame and exceeded their expectations.
